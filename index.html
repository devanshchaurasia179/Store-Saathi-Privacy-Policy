Store Saathi (“we”, “our”, or “us”) operates the Store Saathi mobile application (the “App”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information to provide core app functionality:

a) Personal Information

  • Mobile phone number
  • Shop name and owner name
  • Store details entered by the user

b) Business Data

  • Billing and transaction records
  • Inventory and product details
  • Customer and supplier ledger data

c) Device & App Information

  • Device type and operating system
  • App usage data for performance and security monitoring

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Authenticate users and secure accounts.
  • Enable core features like billing, inventory management, ledgers, and analytics.
  • Sync data seamlessly across multiple devices.
  • Improve performance and enhance the overall user experience.
  • Provide support and resolve technical issues.

3. Data Storage & Security

Secure Storage: All user data is stored on secure servers.

Protection: Data is protected using industry-standard security practices and encryption where applicable.

Integrity: Sensitive operations are protected with robust authentication mechanisms.

Privacy First: We do not sell or share user data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal or business data with third parties, except:

  • Legal Compliance: When required by law or to comply with valid legal obligations.
  • Protection: To protect the rights, property, or safety of Store Saathi, our users, or the public.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

Retention: User data is retained only as long as your account remains active.

Deletion: Users may request data deletion at any time by contacting our support team.

Permanence: Upon a verified request, all associated personal and business data will be permanently deleted from our servers.

6. Children’s Privacy

Store Saathi is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

7. User Rights

  • Access: The right to access the data we hold about you.
  • Correction: The right to update or correct inaccurate information.
  • Erasure: The right to request the deletion of your account and all associated data.
